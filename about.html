About Us

About Free PDF Flash

Free PDF Flash is a passion project born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to powerful document tools — without paying subscription fees, signing up for accounts, or worrying about file privacy.

Our Mission

We believe that essential productivity tools should be free and accessible to all. Our mission is to provide the most comprehensive suite of PDF utilities that runs entirely in your browser. No servers, no uploads, no tracking — just pure, client-side power at your fingertips.

How It Works

Every tool in Free PDF Flash leverages modern browser APIs and JavaScript libraries like jsPDF, pdf-lib, and PDF.js to process your files locally. When you convert an image to PDF, merge documents, or add a watermark, the entire process happens on your device. Your files never leave your computer or phone.

What We Offer

  • Image to PDF Converter — Turn JPG, PNG, WEBP images into polished PDF documents.
  • Bulk Image Converter — Batch-convert large numbers of images to individual PDFs.
  • Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDF files into one, with drag-to-reorder support.
  • Split PDF — Extract specific page ranges from any PDF.
  • Compress PDF — Reduce file sizes with multiple compression levels.
  • Protect PDF — Encrypt your PDFs with password protection.
  • Watermark PDF — Add custom text watermarks to every page.
  • Resume Builder — Build a professional resume and export it as a PDF.
  • Portfolio Generator — Create beautiful portfolio PDFs from images and text.

Our Values

Privacy First: We will never collect, store, or transmit your files. Period.

Free Forever: Our core tools will always remain free. We sustain the project through optional premium features and non-intrusive advertisements.

Community Driven: We listen to user feedback and continuously improve our tools based on real-world needs.

Accessibility: Our tools are designed to work on any device — desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones — including older devices with limited connectivity.

Technology

Free PDF Flash is built with modern web technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript. We use Progressive Web App (PWA) technology so you can install the app and use it offline. The application is compatible with free hosting platforms and can even run inside Android WebView apps.
