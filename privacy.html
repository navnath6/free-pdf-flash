Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

Free PDF Flash ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our website handles information when you use our services.

No File Collection

This is the most important thing to know: we do not collect, upload, store, or transmit any files you process using our tools. All file processing — including converting, merging, splitting, compressing, protecting, and watermarking — happens entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.

Information We May Collect

While we do not collect your files, the website may collect minimal information through:

  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand general usage patterns (page views, popular tools, device types). This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Advertising: If advertisements are displayed, third-party ad networks may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.
  • Contact Form: If you submit a message through our contact form, your name, email, and message are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. On static hosting, this data stays on your device.

Local Storage

Our website uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Conversion statistics and usage streak
  • Achievement badges
  • Download history
  • Contact form submissions

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's storage for this site.

Cookies

Our website itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services (such as ad networks or analytics providers) may set cookies in your browser. You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We load the following external resources via CDN:

  • Font Awesome (icons)
  • Google Fonts (typography)
  • jsPDF, pdf-lib, PDF.js (PDF processing libraries)

These CDN providers may log standard web request data (IP address, user agent) as part of their normal operations. We have no control over their data practices.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Since all file processing occurs locally and we do not store your files on any server, the primary security of your data depends on your own device and browser security. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
